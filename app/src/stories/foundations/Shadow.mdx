# Shadow Tokens

Pixdone uses **soft shadows** for general elevation and **pixel shadows** for retro emphasis. Use pixel shadows selectively for primary CTAs and raised retro cards; not every surface needs a strong pixel shadow. Reward glows are for success/reward feedback.
